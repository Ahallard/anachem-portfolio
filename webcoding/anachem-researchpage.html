Anachem project

we started off our research with a problem statement and user needs statement.
This helped understand the problem from the potenial users view, which is that the user needs a tool to quickly and effectively document customers, orders, order statis and how work they have done.

Then moved onto competitive analysis, user personas and user stories to understand who the users are, and how others handled this problem.
we then moved onto empathy maps and low fidelity wireframes.
